How GRIME works
From watershed
to clean water.
01 — Analyze
Watershed Intelligence
Digital elevation models processed through pysheds hydrological algorithms to identify where trash naturally concentrates — before it ever reaches the ocean.
02 — Deploy
Optimal Placement
Sites scored by generation rate, flow velocity, catchment area, and environmental justice index. Deploy collection units precisely where the math says it matters most.
03 — Collect
Autonomous Action
Computer vision classifies debris in real time. The unit fills autonomously, pings operators via LoRa, and resets — closing the loop on waterway trash at scale.
